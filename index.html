Tribute To Michael Jackson

Musical Genius & Sensation

Michael was an American singer, songwriter, dancer, and philanthropist. Known as the "King of Pop", he is regarded as one of the most significant cultural figures of the 20th century. Jackson influenced my life as a child growing up. Michael said and I quote, 'You never know how long you have with someone. so don't forget to say I love you while you can.'
